Students-Achievement

The ICPC World Finals 2024, held in Astana, Kazakhstan, featured a team from Shahjalal University of Science and Technology (SUST), named SUST_GuessForces, according to Shahjalal University of Science and Technology. The team consisted of Md Shajalal Sohag, Saikat Hosen, and Mozaddede Alfeh Sani, with A. K. M. Fakhrul Hossain as their coach. They qualified for the finals by achieving 2nd place in the ICPC Dhaka Site Regional and 3rd place in the ICPC Asia West Continent Final.

Debojoti Das Soumya just created history and won the first ever IOI gold medal for Bangladesh! The astronomical rise of this kid is beyond anything that we've ever seen in this country.

Congratulations to Team SUST_Gnptrvscwlk from Shahjalal University of Science and Technology (SUST)! They achieved Runner-Up position at the Uttara University Inter-University Programming Contest 2025. Their outstanding problem-solving skills and teamwork truly stood out. Both teams showcased the strength and talent of SUST in the national programming arena. Their determination and teamwork were truly commendable. Wishing them even greater achievements in the future!
